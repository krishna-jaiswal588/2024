17 April 2024 - Ek Yaadgar Pal
Tumhe pheli baar dekhna yaad hai..
Phir tumse pheli baar baat krna bhi yaad hai...
Yaad hai tumhare intzaar me raaton me jaagna...
Or tumhe dekhne k liye intzaar krna bhi ...
Tumhare saath hasna bhi yaad hai,
Tumhare liye rona bhi yaad hai...
Tumhe sab kuch batana bhi yaad hai,
Or bahut kuch chupana bhi....
Yaad hai tumhari aankhein or tumhari khushboo bhi...
Tumhari hasi bhi, tumhari awaaz bhi..
Yaad hai tumhe dur se dekhna or tumhare pass hone ka ehsaas bhi...
Tumhe rooj yaad krna bhi yaad hai
or roj tumhe bulane ki koshish krna bhi...
Tumhe milne ki khushi bhi yaad hai
or tumhe na milne ka gham bhi ....
